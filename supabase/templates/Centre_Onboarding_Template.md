## Section 6 — Policies

*Please share all your policies below. Parents always ask about these before enrolling, so the more complete your answers, the fewer questions they will have.*

*To help you get started, parents typically ask about:*

- **Fees and payment** — when is payment due? what methods do you accept? (PayNow / cash / bank transfer)
- **Makeup and replacement classes** — do you offer them? any conditions or limits?
- **Minimum commitment** — month-to-month, per term, no lock-in?
- **Notice period** — how much notice do you need if a student wants to stop?
- **Refund policy** — if a student drops out mid-term or mid-month?
- **Class size** — how many students are typically in a class?

*If you have any other policies not listed above (e.g. late arrival policy, phone/device rules, sibling discounts, attire requirements, how the trial-to-enrolment process works), please include them too.*

# PART 2 — TRIAL SLOT AVAILABILITY

*Send this separately whenever you have trial slots available. You do not need to fill in Part 1 again.*

---

## How to share your trial slots

Just WhatsApp or message us your available slots in any format — we handle the rest.

For each slot, include:

1. **Programme name** *(what you call the class)*
2. **Who it's for** *(age group, school level, or skill level)*
3. **Date**
4. **Start and end time**
5. **Trial fee** *($)*
6. **Maximum number of students** in the trial session
7. **Any notes for parents** *(what to bring, what to wear, anything to prepare)*

---

## Example message you can send us:

> Saturday 15 March, 9–10am — Wushu Foundations for kids aged 6–9, $25, max 4 students. Wear sports attire and sports shoes.
>
> Same day, 10:30am–12pm — Wushu for older beginners aged 10–17, $25, max 6.
>
> Saturday 22 March, 10–11:30am — Lion Dance trial, open to all beginners aged 8–17, $20, max 6. Tell them to come in groups — it's more fun!

That is all we need. Any format is fine — list, paragraph, voice note, even a photo of a handwritten schedule. We will take it from there.

---

## How often should you send us updates?

Whenever you have new slots available or existing slots change. There is no fixed schedule — just message us and we will update your listing within 24 hours.
